# O'Reilly Auto Parts
Senior Software Developer

**Oct 2017 – Jul 2022** | Remote

Retail-systems modernization at scale. The centerpiece was a customer-pricing rewrite that
moved the pricing data layer off a legacy AS/400 (iSeries) system onto Oracle.

---

## Responsibilities

**Pricing migration**: Migrated 2 billion rows from the legacy AS/400 system to Oracle,
using PL/SQL collections, pipelined functions, parallelism, and bulk processing.

**Nightly validation**: Designed and led an overnight validation-and-correction job
processing billions of pricing rows nightly; managed the team and provided agile guidance.

**Greenfield applications**: Built a full-stack internal time-tracking application from
the ground up (Oracle, Java, Aurelia), and a greenfield internal app for in-store signage
creation, store assignment, and supporting batch jobs.

**Team leadership**: Served as Scrum Master and full-stack developer; led agile
pre-planning for a merchandising initiative.
