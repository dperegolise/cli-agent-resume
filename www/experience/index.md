# Experience

About fifteen years of professional software development across healthcare, nonprofit,
retail, telecom, and consulting. Mostly data-intensive enterprise systems, end to end.

---

## Career arc

I started self-taught: as a number portability analyst at Syniverse, I learned PHP on the
job and automated an estimated 12% of the service center's manual workload, which turned
into a developer role. From there: big-data analytics for the major U.S. mobile carriers,
consulting engagements, and a series of senior full-stack positions in retail, nonprofit,
and healthcare.

Most of it has been enterprise modernization: replacing legacy systems, migrating and
optimizing large datasets, and automating mission-critical workflows. Along the way I've
acted as technical lead and Scrum Master across multiple teams. Sprint planning, mentoring,
and production incident response are as much a part of the job as the code.
